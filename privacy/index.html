Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

CyberFreq ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CyberFreq mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in through Tesla's OAuth system, we receive your Tesla account identifier, vehicle identification number (VIN), and vehicle model. We do not receive or store your Tesla account password.

Location Data: When the App is active, we collect your device's GPS coordinates to provide proximity-based features. Location data is used in real time to match you with nearby drivers and is not stored permanently on our servers.

Voice Data: Push-to-talk voice transmissions are relayed in real time to nearby users through our voice service provider (Agora). Voice data is not recorded or stored by us.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as session duration, features used, and app performance metrics to improve the App.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your Tesla vehicle ownership and create your profile
  • To provide proximity-based driver matching and voice chat
  • To display your car model on your profile (visible to nearby users)
  • To manage channels and community features
  • To process premium subscriptions
  • To improve the App and fix bugs
  • To communicate with you about updates and features

Information Sharing

With Nearby Users: Your chosen display name, car model, approximate distance, and heading direction are visible to other CyberFreq users within your proximity range. Your exact GPS coordinates are never shared with other users.

Service Providers: We use third-party services including Agora (voice chat infrastructure) and Tesla Fleet API (vehicle verification). These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

Account information is retained while your account is active. Location data is processed in real time and not stored permanently. Voice transmissions are not recorded. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Withdraw location permissions at any time through your device settings

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
